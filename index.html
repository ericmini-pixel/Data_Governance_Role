Data Governance · Core Concept
What is Data Governance?
Google
"…everything you do to ensure data is secure, private, accurate, available, and usable…"
Microsoft
"…system of internal policies organisations use to manage, access, and secure enterprise data."
1
Set the Rules
Policy Management
Defines how data is collected, stored & used.
💡 "No data leaves our servers without encryption."
2
Assign Ownership
Data Stewardship
One accountable person per data domain.
💡 "The CRM Manager owns all customer data."
3
Enforce Standards
Data Quality Mgmt
Monitors accuracy, completeness & consistency.
💡 "Order fields must pass format validation before executed."
